About the app

What is Prevue?

PreVue is a new kind of dating app that ditches the
doctored photos and misleading bios. With Prevue,
you match based on a short video that shows who
you really are no filters or uploads allowed.

We believe that videos tell more about a person
than a photo or bio box ever could. Seeing someone
move and listening to what they have chosen to
share about themselves is the ultimate vibe check.

You deserve to be choosy about your next partner
and to have them embrace your real self from the
start. Know before you go, with PreVue.
